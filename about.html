Who are we?

We are a group of high school & college students, trying to make an impact by making education more accessible.

Artificial Intelligence is a rapidly growing technology that is being implemented into various industries

We seek to address the issue of limited exposure to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and understanding of AI concepts among students, hindering their ability to navigate through latest technology & its benefits.
